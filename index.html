How to Make Sushi

Description
The cheap and easy alternative to dining out is making your own sushi!
This recipe will change the way you think about the Japanese Cuisine.
Ingredients
  • 2 cups of cooked sushi rice
  • 4 strips of nori seaweed
  • 1 bamboo mat (for rolling)1 cup of avocado, cut in horizontal strips
  • 1 cup cucumber, cut in horizontal strips
Directions
  1. Lay out bamboo mat on a flat surface
  2. Place one sheet of nori on the bamboo mat
  3. Spread rice out to cover whole sheet
  4. Place strips of filling in the middle
  5. Roll the bamboo sheet
  6. Open bamboo sheet back up, and take out the rolled sushi
  7. Cut the long sushi into pieces, and serve
